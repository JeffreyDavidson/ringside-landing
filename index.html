FAQ

Got Questions?

Who is Ringside built for? +

Independent wrestling promoters who run live shows. Whether you're booking a monthly show at a local venue or running weekly events across multiple locations, Ringside gives you the tools to manage it all professionally.

Can I import my existing roster data? +

Yes. All plans support CSV imports, and Federation tier includes hands-on data migration assistance. We'll help you get your spreadsheets into Ringside without losing anything.

Is there a free trial? +

Every plan comes with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required. Get in, set up your promotion, and see if Ringside is right for you before paying a cent.

What does "founding price" mean? +

Early adopters who sign up during launch get a permanently discounted rate. As long as you keep your subscription active, you'll never pay the regular price. It's our way of saying thanks to the promoters who believe in us from day one.

Can I manage multiple shows or brands? +

The Federation plan supports multiple brands under one promotion — think separate rosters, titles, and events for each brand. Starter and Promoter plans cover a single brand.

Is my data safe? +

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, backed up daily, and isolated to your account. We'll never share or sell your promotion's information.
